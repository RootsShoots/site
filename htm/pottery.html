 A very buoyant market and one that leans towards fashion trends.

We are a main supplier for Woodlodge products who are one of the largest in the industry.

 

At Roots & Shoots you will find one of the largest pot displays in the county and as such our stock is constantly changing and the prices are unbelievably low, with buy 1 get 1 free occasionally on offer.

We also carry a wide range of plastic planting containers in a wide variety of shapes and sizes.

For that rustic look how about some genuine malt whisky barrels as planters?

Our barrels come from the WM Grant distilleries (Glenfiddich) but be warned, they do smell very strongly of the golden liquid.

 

There is a huge selection of pottery available in a range of different materials. Choose one to suit the style of your garden and the planting scheme you have in mind.

 

Larger pots hold a lot more compost so produce much better, longer-lasting displays than similar smaller ones. Larger pots are also easier to keep watered, but are more difficult to move around, so bear these points in mind when you buy.

For more inspiration, take a look at our of stone ornaments, sun dials and bird baths.

We have searched extensively to get you quality stoneware at fantastic prices. You won't believe the prices.

Not only do these features add beauty to the garden, they also make excellent gifts that are functional and long lasting.
