 At Roots and Shoots we carry a large selection of wild bird care products including:-Fatballs, Seed Feeders, Peanut Feeders, Tubular Feeders, Bird Tables and Nesting Boxes. Also stocked is great variety of seeds and nuts to keep plenty of birds happy.

Our seeds are sold in in packs from 1Kg up to 25Kgs. The Larger sizes offer our customers excellent value for money.

Our Wild Bird Care section has continued to grow with an ever increasing demand for this product range.

Lawn Care

We stock a large range of lawn care products, to give your garden that professional look.

On our shelves we supply products such as Miracle-Gro, Evergreen and Levington, and have a range which will cater for all your needs. Use our spreaders to give your garden that quick but accurate application of fertilizers or grass seed.

We also stock an excellent range of insecticides and fungicides to keep your garden looking great along with composts, soil improvers and mulches you will be the envy of your neighbours.

We also have a great selection of weed killers available. We supply Roundup, Weedol and Pathclear products which are available in ready to use spray guns or sachets       (which require dilution) and are rain proof in 10 minutes!
