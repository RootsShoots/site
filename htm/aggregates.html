The use of aggregates in the garden has grown dramatically over the last few years. Whether it be for paths, driveways or on the garden.

We stock a large range of all types of aggregates in easy to handle 25kg bags.

Our most popular range is welsh slate chippings in plum green or blue to compliment the appearance of your garden and also to assist with weed control.

We also stock a range of large rocks and stones for building that rockery you have always wanted.
